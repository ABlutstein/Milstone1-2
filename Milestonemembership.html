Membership

The UKSMUA sits at the core of engineering based manufacturing and as an association works tirelessly to ensure member companies are as commercially successful as possible. Membership of the UKSMUA is open to all manufacturers and users of industrial digitalisation technology and services to help the UK innovate and advance towards a smart manufacturing future. Become a part of the Association and you’ll not only have access to our support and advice services you’ll also find a host of additional benefits.
In short the UKSMUA

1. Uniquely represents smart engineering based manufacturing

2. Is focused on supporting both manufacturers and users of industrial smart technology

3. Provides relevant and specific industry intelligence and technical data

4. Encourages talent through education and support

There are four types of membership on offer from the UKSMUA – full membership, associate membership, User Partner and Small Business To become a full member of the Association you must fit in to one of the following categories:

1. UK Manufacturers and Users of industrial digitalisation technology and services

2. UK Importers of industrial digitalisation technology and services

3. Equipment suppliers or importers

4. End Users of such technologies

All of the above categories are subject to additional terms and conditions.
Associate membership is open to all companies who do not fit into the above categories but have involvement in the industry – such as trade magazine publishers, banks and finance companies and advisory services. Small Business membership has been set up to small companies that are not eligible for full UKSMUA membership due to their size or turnover. This membership helps such companies to grow and eventually become full members.
